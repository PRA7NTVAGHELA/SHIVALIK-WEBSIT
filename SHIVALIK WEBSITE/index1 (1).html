Water Treatment Plant (WTP)

Si’s has satisfactorily completed WTP with capacity of 2 MLD to 30 MLD at different places of Gujarat and more than 5 years of operation and maintenance has completed. In WTP Water treatment removes contaminants and undesirable components, or reduces their concentration so that the water becomes fit for its desired end-use. This treatment is crucial to human health and allows humans to benefit from both drinking and irrigation use.
